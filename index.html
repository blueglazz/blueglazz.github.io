About Me

I am a PhD candidate in the Department of Mathematical Sciences at Tsinghua University. My advisor is Professor Zhengwei Liu.

Research Interests

My research lies at the intersection of mathematical physics and quantum information theory. I am mainly interested in the interaction between operator algebras, especially subfactor theory, and quantum many-body physics.
